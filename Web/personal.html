Project Maelstrom

Music Courtesy of SHADE08.

Maelstrom is an unrelease prototype of a First Person Adventure Game. It implemments physics based movement controls,
Billboarding sprites, Functiong UI, Health and Ammo systems, and item pickup systems. Many sprites were self made
or resourced from royalty free images, and others are temporary place holder sprites using Duke Nukem 3D Assets.
